MAKE A PROACTIVE PLAY FOR YOUR HEALTH

At Kaiser Permanente, we believe in driving your health forward—that means eating clean, exercising smart, loving more, and living better. Starting good habits isn’t always easy, but the truth is, we all have it in us. Learn how Stephen Curry and everyday Americans get on track towards a healthier lifestyle. And then get yourself moving, because when it comes to your health, offense is the best defense.

stories of

proactive health

anthony

“Your body is an investment. You can be putting deposits in now to pull out later or you can just be spending and spending away and eventually, it’s going to catch up to us.”

For Anthony, staying fit isn’t just an option, it’s a priority. Being proactive about his health helps him live comfortably with a prosthetic limb, so he can take on new challenges every day.

mike

“It’s a numbers game. Eat better. Work out more. Just have your workout burning-calories number higher than your intake.”

Mike proves that you don’t need a nutritionist or a gym membership to take control of your health. He makes proactive plays for a better life in his own living room.

michelle

“With anything, you start with a little tiny step and you work your way up”

Michelle’s daily routine begins with a 20-minute meditation session. Starting her morning off with a little me-time helps her stay balanced and positive through the rest of the day.
