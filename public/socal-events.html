Events

Check out our programs and activities designed to help you be your best in mind, body and spirit. Because when you thrive so does your family, our members and patients, our communities, and our organization. This just might be the start of something healthy.

Kaiser Permanente Kern County offers a wide variety of classes to help you live your best life, including: Ask-A-Dietician, metabolic rate testing, muscle toning, and biofeedback, among many others.

Class times and locations change often, so please make sure to call: 661-664-3712 or
877-524-7373 to schedule your attendance.

Many of the classes are open to the general public. Some classes may require a fee.
